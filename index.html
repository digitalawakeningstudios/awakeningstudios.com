Validate Your Design

Avoid expensive and time-consuming mistakes that eat into profitability.

You can only get so far with schematics, wireframes and descriptions of user interactions. I’m a big fan of creating a working prototype first, so we can iteratively design the UI and UX, and get on the same page with the client. My design sensibilities and art + animation background help me take your experience design to the next level.

Create Data-Driven UI

Empower your client and team with configurable, maintainable code.

Don’t limit yourself to having to loop in the programmer for small changes to the experience. We can use data in a variety of ways to drive the UI, including a CMS, APIs, algorithmic techniques, and simple config files. We’ll make sure the client can directly tweak content and settings where appropriate, while protecting the core experience.
